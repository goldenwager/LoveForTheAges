```
Their Worlds

Where They Come From

Two cultures, thousands of miles apart, each rich with music, flavour, art, and ancient soul — and yet, the universe conspired to bring them together.

🇮🇳
India
Geralt

A man born of vibrant colours, monsoon rains, and centuries of poetry. His roots trace back to a land of chai mornings and starlit festivals — where love is expressed in spices, in music, in devotion that knows no limit.

🇹🇷
Turkey
Adelina

A woman shaped by the Bosphorus breeze and the call of minarets at dusk. She carries in her eyes the depth of a civilisation that bridges East and West — where warmth is measured in hospitality and every meal is an act of love.
```

Their Story

The Journey Together

Every great love story unfolds in moments — here are theirs.

```
The Beginning
A Chance Encounter

They met in the way fate always arranges its most important appointments — unexpectedly, at a moment neither was ready for, yet both somehow always needed. Geralt heard her laugh first, and something ancient recognised something ancient.

First Days
Learning Each Other's World

She tasted biryani for the first time with his family; he stirred his first cup of Turkish coffee and read the grounds like a fortune he already knew. They discovered that love is the oldest translator — it needs no dictionary.

The Middle
Two Cultures, One Kitchen

Their home became a beautiful collision — saffron beside sumac, henna beside the evil eye, Bollywood melodies drifting through rooms lit by Turkish lanterns. They built something entirely new from the best of their worlds.

Always
Still Writing It

The most beautiful chapter is the one they are living now. Every morning they choose each other again — across the distances of language, of geography, of difference — and find that in choosing each other, they are more themselves than ever.

```
"

You did not love me in spite of where I came from. You loved me because of it — and through your eyes, I saw my own home as beautiful for the first time.

— Geralt, to Adelina

What They Share

The Language of Their Love

```
🌹
Devotion

Like the rose in Sufi poetry and the gül in Turkish verse, their love is an offering — given daily, tended with care, thorns and all.

🫖
Warmth

From chai to çay, they speak the same language of warmth. Every cup shared is a small ceremony of belonging, a reminder that they are each other's home.

🌙
Mystery

He finds new galaxies in her eyes. She reads ancient stories in his silence. There is always more to discover — and they are endlessly, delightedly curious.

🎶
Harmony

A tabla rhythm and a saz string. East meeting East, heart meeting heart. Their love is a song composed of two melodies that were always meant to intertwine.

🌺
Beauty

The lotus and the tulip. Two flowers from two gardens — both symbols of love, both turning toward the light. Together they bloom more brilliantly.

✨
Magic

How else to explain it? The mathematics of seven billion people, the geography of continents — and still, somehow, each other. That is not luck. That is written in the stars.
```
